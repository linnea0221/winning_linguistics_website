Is it a language? Sign languages are languages, yes, and every country (pretty much) has its own version, much like the other natural languages of the world. If you are asking whether conlangs are languages, then also yes, although they're obviously not natural languages. Music and other art forms are versions of non-linguistic expressions, and do not constitute a language. Animal grunts and such also don't count as a language but they are still valid forms of communication.
